Tomato pasta bake

Make this simle student pasta and tomato dish for a delicious, filling and budget-friendly. It is a great way to use up simple storecupboard ingredints.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 400g can cherry or chopped tomatoes
  • pinch suger

Preparation

  1. Heat the oil in a medium pan and fry the onionfor 5 mins until sofiened and lightly golden.
  2. Meanwhile, cook the pasta according to pack instucions. Heat the grill to high. Mix together the cheese and breadcrumbs.
  3. Drain the pasta and stir into the sause. Tip in the frankfurters and spoon into a heatproof dish.
